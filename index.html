CGI artist and VFX artist

I was born on January 10, 2005.

✔ I graduated from the Toktogul Satylganov Boarding Lyceum in Karakol, Kyrgyzstan.

✔ I am currently studying Business and IT at Kyrgyz State Technical University.

I’ve been passionate about design and visual effects since I was 12. Now I have over 9 years of experience in CGI, 3D, and VFX.

✔ I worked for a commercial studio for one year, creating visual effects for advertisements and music videos.

✔ Currently, I am working at **MOV.FX Studio**, focusing on visual effects for films and trailers.

My main expertise includes realistic simulations, 3D animation, and compositing. I aim to create visuals that evoke emotion and enhance the story.


I work with the following software: **Blender, Fusion Studio, Nuke, EmberGen, Liquigen, After Effects, Premiere Pro.**

Each project is a new chance to push the limits and leave a strong visual impact.
